# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Create a marketing website for Glow Maxing beauty product that showcases the 7-day results promise, skin benefits, and detailed product information.

**Planned changes:**
- Build hero section with Glow Maxing branding, 7-day results tagline, and call-to-action
- Create product benefits section highlighting facial glow enhancement and blackhead/whitehead removal
- Implement 7-day results timeline showing progression from day 1 to day 7
- Add comprehensive product details section with ingredients, usage instructions, and specifications
- Design beauty-themed interface with warm glowing tones, soft gradients, and elegant typography

**User-visible outcome:** Users can explore the Glow Maxing product through an elegant, informative website that presents the product's benefits, 7-day transformation timeline, and detailed product information in a visually cohesive skincare aesthetic.
